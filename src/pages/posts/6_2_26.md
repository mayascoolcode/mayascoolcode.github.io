***What inspires you to pursue art?***

As a kid I liked fashion, anime, ponies, plushies, spirituality, and anything lavender. Much of my time was spent digitally drawing, editing "aesthetic" images on Picsart, or playing dress up on GirlsGoGames. I liked characters and shows like Monster High, Winx Club, Hello Kitty, Pucca, Betty Boop, and the various ocs of furry artists online. Hentai, ecchi, and sexy media were fascinating to me. I began coding cute websites and linux rices. Hours of my day would go by daydreaming about a kaleidoscopic world with loops of alt hiphop, pop, and R&B songs in my head. I create to transport me back to that feeling.

***Is there a recurring theme or subject in your artwork?***

My work is either cute, kinky, and/or emotional. 

***Who are your biggest influences?***

Junko Minuzo, Chisato Tatsumi, Rossali, Vampkitty2001, Doja Cat, Kali Uchis
