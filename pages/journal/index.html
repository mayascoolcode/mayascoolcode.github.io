1.1.26

Hey, welcome to my little blog. Heres an introduction: I'm Maya, I'm an illustrator and I enjoy making edgy femme drawings. I want to share my art online, but the internet stresses me out. So I made my own website, as an archive and time capsule for myself and my works.


I first began drawing as a child, a Sonic fanartist. I remember drawing art of Cream and Blaze, I really liked them. Of course as I got older I was introduced to anime and animation memes, and so I started drawing thick furries as a teenager. I started having crazy artblock later on, and would draw a lot of humans. But now, I've found myself at an equilibrium, drawing cute kemonomimi art (more human-ish characters with animal characteristics). I love "girly" things, dark palletes, and textures.


As I enter 2026, I want to relax and learn to really enjoy creating again, I've had a very hard time because of pride and perfectionism. I just want to relax now and not take it too serious. I've never seriously shown my art to a audience or anything like that, so I'm gonna take advantage of that fact and allow myself to be silly. A couple more projects I have besides that, is an EP and a cute dress up game. I want to connect to the things I enjoyed doing as a kid, like playing GirlsGoGames and animatics.


2025 was very blah for me. It felt just, like a waste of time, as I once again let my fear get in the way of many things. But there were some good things that have happened for me in the end. I graduated community college and I've found a lovely person despite being hopeless, and having basically zero energy. It was not all bad, at all. I'm excited to see what happens this year.
